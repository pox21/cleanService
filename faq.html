Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • What services does your company offer?

    - We specialize in professional upholstery, carpet, and mattress cleaning services for both private and commercial clients.

  • How often should I clean my upholstery and carpets?

    - For residential spaces, we recommend annual cleaning. Commercial spaces with higher foot traffic may benefit from more frequent cleaning, such as every 6 months.

  • What is the process of your cleaning services?

    - Our process typically includes pre-inspection, pre-treatment, cleaning, and post-cleaning inspection. We use advanced techniques and eco-friendly products for optimal results.

  • Do you provide services for commercial spaces?

    - Yes, we offer tailored cleaning solutions for commercial establishments, including offices, hotels, restaurants, and more.

  • How long does it take for upholstery or carpets to dry after cleaning?

    - Drying times vary but typically range from 4-6 hours. We also offer quick-drying options for clients who need to use their space promptly.

  • Can your cleaning services remove tough stains?

    - Our professional cleaning methods are designed to tackle a wide range of stains effectively. While we cannot guarantee complete removal in every case, our team works diligently to achieve the best results.

  • Are your cleaning products safe for children and pets?

    - Yes, we prioritize the use of safe and eco-friendly cleaning products, ensuring the well-being of your family, including children and pets.

  • How can I schedule a cleaning service?

    - Scheduling is easy! Contact our team, and we will arrange a convenient time for our experts to visit your location.

  • Do you offer discounts for commercial cleaning services?

    - Yes, we provide customized pricing for commercial clients based on the scale and frequency of services. Contact our consultants to discuss your specific needs.

  • What sets your company apart from others in the industry?

    - We take pride in our experienced team, cutting-edge equipment, eco-friendly practices, and a commitment to delivering exceptional results for both private and commercial clients.
