Upholstery

Upholstery cleaning

Optimize your time and effort by entrusting the challenge of stains and dirt on your sofa upholstery to the professionals. Tackling these issues independently can prove to be a formidable task. Common household cleaning agents often fall short, leaving behind white streaks and stubborn stains on the upholstery in a majority of cases. Experience the difference with 'CleanForAll'—where furniture care meets expertise. Our skilled professionals are adept at swiftly eliminating stains from diverse sources, removing grime, and neutralizing unpleasant odors, ensuring your sofa undergoes a rejuvenating transformation.

The furniture cleaning process consists of five stages:
  • * Dry cleaning with a vacuum cleaner.
  • * Stain removal.
  • * Application of professional chemicals.
  • * Furniture cleaning with a professional extractor.
  • * Furniture cleaning with a conditioner-rinse.

The sofa typically dries within 6-10 hours at room temperature, but as a complimentary service to all our clients, we offer express drying, ensuring your sofa dries rapidly
